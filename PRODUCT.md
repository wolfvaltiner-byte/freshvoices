# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Potential clients seeking a professional voice-over artist: marketing managers, agency producers, e-learning teams, audiobook publishers, and production companies. They arrive with a project in mind, want to hear samples fast, assess fit, and reach out — typically on desktop during work hours, sometimes mobile.

## Product Purpose
Showcase Wolf Valtiner's voice-over work, demonstrate range across genres (corporate, documentary, e-learning, commercials, audiobooks, IVR), and convert visitors into enquiries. Success = a visitor hears samples, feels confident in the quality, and submits a project request.

## Brand Personality
Professional, fresh, approachable. Competent and trustworthy without being stiff or corporate. Modern energy that reflects a creative profession. Easy to work with — the site should feel like talking to a real person, not a faceless agency.

## Anti-references
- Generic stock-photo agency sites with templated layouts
- Over-designed creative portfolios that prioritise flash over usability
- Corporate voice-over directories that feel like databases rather than personal brands
- Wix/Squarespace template sites where every voice artist looks the same

## Design Principles
1. **Sound first** — audio samples are the core product; every page should make listening effortless and immediate
2. **Show range, not volume** — demonstrate versatility across genres without overwhelming; quality of curation over quantity of clips
3. **Lower the barrier** — from first visit to enquiry should feel frictionless; no gates, no forms before listening, clear next steps
4. **Creative but grounded** — the site should feel like a creative professional's space, not a tech startup or corporate brochure
5. **Bilingual without friction** — DE/EN switching should be seamless and never break the experience

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. Standard contrast ratios, keyboard navigation, screen reader support for audio controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion alternatives for animations.
